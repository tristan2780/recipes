Ramyun

A classic Korean dish!

This is a go to Korean dish that is easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Ramyun (Shin brand is best)
  • Water
  • Pot

Recipe instructions

  1. Boil water in pot
  2. Once water is boiling, add Ramyun and sauce pack
  3. Stir around until noodles cooked
  4. Enjoy tasty meal!
